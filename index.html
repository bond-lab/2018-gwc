GWC 2018

The 9th Global WordNet Conference

8 – 12 January, 2018

Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore

Welcome to the 9th Global WordNet Conference (GWC 2018)!

This is an opportunity for researchers and developers to present and discuss their latest results on the development, enrichment and exploitation of wordnets for various languages around the world. This conference is hosted by the Computational Linguistics Lab at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore and the Global WordNet Association.

Registration [↑ Top]

Registration is now open.

  • Early bird registration! (until November 25th).
    • At least one author of each paper should be registered before November 25th for the paper to be accepted in the proceedings.
  • Online registration will be open until January 1st 2018.
  • We will also accept on site registration (cash: 250 Singapore Dollars; Banquet: SGD 30).
  • Registration includes membership in the Global WordNet Association.
  • As a CLASS supported event, registration is free for NTU students and staff (except for the banquet: SGD 30).
